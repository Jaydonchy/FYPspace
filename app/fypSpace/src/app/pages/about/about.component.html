Aims:

The research aims to uncover relevant information and developing a user-centric productive FYP Management system for the volatile switch between blended learning and pure e-learning for different roles.

Objectives:

FYPSpace System Allows

Project Managers to Match Student with Supervisors and Second Marker

Lecturers to track their workload

Lecturers to approve Project Proposal Form and Project Specification Form

Upcoming Features

Student to Submit and Edit their Project Proposal Form and Project Specifciation Form

Student to Select Question from a question bank

Student and Lecturer to Manage their Meeting Logs
