Aims:

To facilitate project management responsibilities carried out by RMCT students, Internship students, final year project students (FYP), Deans, HR, Head of schools (HOS), Academic leaders(AL), FYP supervisors, FYP second markers, project administrators (FYP), project managers (FYP) and database administrator (DBA).

FYP Bank System Allows

RMCT Students , to search for and select the best FYP proposals, submit/resubmit their draft proposal and propose the name of a maximum of 3 supervisors for their FYP

FYP Students, to search for and select the best FYP proposals, submit/resubmit their PPF (Project Proposal Form) and PSF (Project Specification Form) and view the approval status of their PPF and PSF

FYP Supervisors, to upload new FYP proposals, monitor the usage of their proposals, approve the PPF and PSF of their supervisees and monitor the progress of their supervisees in their FYP

FYP Second markers, to upload new FYP proposals, monitor the usage of their proposals, mark the PSF of their advisees and monitor the progress of advisees in their FYP

FYPC, to review each proposal submitted by supervisors and update the approval status of proposal with comments

FYP Administrators, to manage the details of FYP students

FYP Project managers , to upload new FYP proposals, monitor the usage of their proposals, approve the PPF of supervisees, mark the PSF of their advisees, monitor the progress of supervisees in their FYP, assign and reassign suitable supervisor and second marker for each RMCT amd FYP students

Database administrator, to maintain the FYPBaNK System to ensure that the FYPBANK is UP for 24/7
